Home-Made Artisan Cakes

We are an artisan bakery working out of a large Victorian railway arch at Temple Meads station in Bristol. We specialise in sourdough, handmade pastries, seasonal cakes and savouries. Within one open space all of our products are prepared, baked and sold.
